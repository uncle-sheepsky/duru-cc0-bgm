🎵 **9 DURU tracks — now released CC0 (free, no attribution required)**

I've put **9** original background-music tracks from my DURU videos into the **public domain (CC0 1.0)**.
Use them for **anything** — videos, games, streams, commercial projects. Attribution welcome, never required.

🎹 Every track is **composed & synthesized in code** (Python + NumPy/SciPy), no DAW.
Genres: kawaii future-bass · lo-fi · chiptune/arcade · rhythm-game · denpa · 2048 & Tetris arcade.

📦 Download (FLAC lossless + MP3 320 kbps):
https://github.com/uncle-sheepsky/duru-cc0-bgm/releases/tag/v1

More tracks coming. If you like them, a channel sub means a lot 🐑
